Connor's Budget

Give us your budget and we will give you the best options you can afford!

Role: UI/UX designer

Tools: Figma/ Miro

Time: 3 weeks

Design Challenge

Even before the onset of the pandemic, young travellers have been frustrated that they have been unable to research and book trips based on their budget. They want a search tool that offers creative trip suggestions based on a strict budget cap with no hidden fees and unexpected charges.

Solution

Connor's Budget is an app that helps travelers research and book trips based on the total value that they want to spend. The concept is for the search functionality to start with the budget and then recommend proposals for destinations, accommodations, and means of transportation to provide the user with creative trip proposals. As with other travel search engines, this app would have the technology to serve as the perfect tool for planning, rearching, and fully booking all aspects of a trip, along with giving recommendations and information on restrictions resulting from COVID-19. Finally, this app will also provide suggestions for authentic local experiences.

User Research
Research Objetive
Interviews
Our team is focused on the community of travelers who have had to put their lives on hold, who need a new platform to plan their trips --big or small-- as soon we’re all clear to fly
Our team conducted a total of 9 user interviews to determine interests in traveling after the global pandemic. We took our findings and constructed an affinity map, an empathy map, and a user persona based on these real users, to determine real needs.
Research Objetive
Interviews
Our user persona would like to plan his trip easier according to his quote, without spending more money than he is planning. He would love to explore the area to know the history and discover the local culture, the most important for him is to avoid waste in case he gets lost and to avoid places that are closed or require prior booking, and learn about the new limitations that relate to the pandemic.
According to our interviews the main fear is to waste time and money while traveling, they feel frustrated traveling without knowledge about the limitations in the areas that they would like to explore. The biggest problem that they have had before covid is they spend more money that they sometimes are planning to spend a specified amount, and the time that they spend planning to visit areas, but wasting time upon arrival when they have to figure out traveling methodology.

Competitor Analysis
In general, all of these competitors have a good trip planning service for a complete package. You can search for flights, hotels, and cars, all with the same destination and dates. The results can be filtered by popularity, price, amenities and much more, but your trip would depend on the prices they offer you, none of them can be adapted to your budget.

Conclusion
In the beginning of the project, we wanted to focus on concerns related to COVID for travelers. However, after all the interviews and the entire investigation, we realized for our target COVID is not one of main pain points. Ultimately, we concluded that for our target (people in their 20s), the main point of opportunity is to offer a service that enables users to easily find interesting travel options with a low budget. One challenge throughout the process was parsing out my own preferences and interests from the needs of the average user for this type of app. In addition, I learned that every user has different needs, and because of the diversity of interests, it was challenging to firmly establish and define the main target. Finally, although COVID information for traveling is currently only part of one of our features, we know from our surveys and research that in the future we can further develop and improve this functionality as we know our target would find that useful.
